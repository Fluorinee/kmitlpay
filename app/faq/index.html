About the Service
  • What is KMITL Pay Servies?

    KMITL Pay Servies is a safe, convenient online platform for KMITL Bank customers to perform banking transactions

    or to pay the bills via the Internet. There is no extra charge for using this platform in case of PromptPay users

    however , normal product fees and charges continue to apply, if any.

  • Why should I KMITL Pay Servies?

    It's safe. You can now bank with confidence knowing that your transactions remain safe with us using our Online Banking platform.

    KMITL Pay Servies maintains world class security standards on our Online Banking platform.

    It's convenient. You can perform banking transactions from the comfort of your home or office, anywhere in the world, anytime, with our 24 by 7 service. No special software is required.

    As long as you have a personal computer (PC) with the minimum configuration, you are ready to start.

Applying for KMITL Pay Servies
  • Who can apply for this service?

    Any KMITL Bank customer with a Savings Account can apply for KMITL Pay Servies.

  • How do I apply for this service?

    There is 2 step to register for KMITL Pay Servies:

    1. Get OTP - go to KMITL Bank website > Login your bank account > Profile > get otp
    2. Register - Using your Account Number & OTPPassword. you must register in KMITL Pay application.

  • What do I need in order to use this service?

    You need to know your Account ID & OTPPassword to register KMITL Pay Servies.

    Actually, you need a PC with internet access and for receiving the One Time Passcode (OTP) via bank website.
